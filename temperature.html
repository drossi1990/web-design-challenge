Latitude vs. Temperature

Comparing latitude to temperature reveals two interesting trends.

First, it is clear that temperatures trend upwards towards the equator, and downwards towards the upper latitudes.

Second, while you might expect the left arm of the graph to complete a "U" shape towards the lower latitudes, the lack of habitable land in the geographic south has created a hole in the data.
